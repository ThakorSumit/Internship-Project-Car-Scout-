Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

1. Information We Collect

When you register on CarScout, we collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, and location. Sellers additionally provide vehicle details, images, and pricing information for their listings.

We also automatically collect usage data such as pages visited, search queries, filters applied, and device/browser information to improve our platform experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your CarScout account
  • To facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers
  • To send price alert notifications you have subscribed to
  • To process payments securely via Razorpay
  • To generate AI-powered vehicle inspection reports
  • To send transactional emails (offer updates, test drive confirmations)
  • To improve platform features and user experience

3. Payments & Financial Data

CarScout uses Razorpay as our payment gateway. We do not store your card details, UPI credentials, or net banking information on our servers. All payment data is handled directly by Razorpay in accordance with PCI-DSS standards. We only retain transaction references and amounts for record-keeping purposes.

4. AI Inspection Reports

Vehicle data submitted by sellers is processed by Google's Gemini AI to generate inspection reports. This data is transmitted to Google's API under their privacy policy. AI-generated reports are advisory only and do not constitute a professional mechanical inspection. CarScout is not liable for decisions made based on AI report outputs.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Groq API — for AI inspection reports
  • Other users — buyer/seller contact details shared only when a transaction is initiated

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, personal data is removed within 30 days. Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting compliance. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-transactional emails at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (may limit platform access)

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries or data requests, contact us at privacy@carscout.in. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
